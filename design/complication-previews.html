accessoryRectangular

Modular main slot, Smart Stack, wide corners. ~356×140pt canvas.

Icon pill on the left, two lines of text on the right. Workout-green adds a stroke to the icon.

accessoryCorner

Infograph curved corner slots. Weather-style layout: the points sit straight at the corner apex, and the step count curves along the bezel via widgetLabel.

bottom-right corner (nudge-zone red)

accessoryInline

Single text line above the clock digits. Apple strips custom colour — always renders as the face's accent.

Two forms: plain "N steps - M pts", or on a workout-green day the "(workout)" suffix.
